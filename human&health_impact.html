Human Health Impact

Microplastics in Humans

Microplastics from polluted water and seafood can enter the human body through everyday food and drink. Scientists are still researching their impact, but they may accumulate in the body and affect long-term health.

Harmful Chemicals from Plastic

Plastic can release toxic chemicals like BPA. These chemicals enter food and water. They may affect hormones. This can impact growth and health.

Air Pollution from Burning Plastic

Burning plastic creates toxic smoke. People breathe in harmful particles. This can damage the lungs. It may cause asthma and breathing problems.
