7 Main Types of Plastic Waste

1. PET (Polyethylene)

Found in: Water and soda bottles. Most commonly recycled.

2. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene)

Found in: Milk jugs and detergent bottles. Very sturdy.

3. PVC (Polyvinyl)

Found in: Pipes and window frames. Difficult to recycle.

4. LDPE (Low-Density)

Found in: Grocery bags and bubble wrap. Soft and flexible.

5. PP (Polypropylene)

Found in: Yogurt tubs and straws. Safe for high heat.

6. PS (Polystyrene)

Found in: Takeaway boxes and Styrofoam. Hard to recycle.

7. Other Plastics

Found in: Baby bottles and fiberglass. A mix of plastics.
