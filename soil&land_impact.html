Soil & Land Impact

Soil Pollution from Plastic Waste

Plastic waste stays in the soil for a long time. It breaks into microplastics. These particles pollute the land and reduce soil quality.

Reduced Soil Fertility and Plant Growth

Microplastics in soil can stop water and nutrients from moving properly. This makes it harder for plants to grow. It can reduce crop production over time.

Long-Term Land
Degradation

Plastic pollution builds up over time. It weakens soil health and fertility. This can damage land for future use.
