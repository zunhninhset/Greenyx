Plastic Problems

Explore the 8 major ways plastic waste is impacting our world today.

Overuse of Single-Use Plastics

The widespread use of disposable plastics creates large amounts of daily waste in cities.

Plastic Litter in Streets and Drains

Improperly discarded plastic blocks drainage systems and increases the risk of flooding.

River and Ocean Pollution

Plastic waste pollutes rivers and oceans, harming water quality and marine ecosystems.

Harm to Wildlife and Biodiversity

Animals and sea creatures are often injured or killed by plastic pollution.

Air Pollution from Burning Plastic

Burning plastic releases toxic gases that damage air quality and human health.

Lack of Recycling Awareness

Limited knowledge about recycling leads to higher plastic waste and environmental pollution.

Heavy Dependence on Plastic in Daily Life

Modern lifestyles rely heavily on plastic packaging and disposable products for convenience.

Slow Decomposition of Plastic

Plastic takes hundreds of years to decompose, causing long-term environmental problems.
