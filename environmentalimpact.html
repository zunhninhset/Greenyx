Enviromental Impact

Environmental Pollution

Plastic waste builds up in public areas such as streets and parks, causing pollution and harming the environment. Since it decomposes very slowly, it remains in the land for many years.

Littering in public places

Littering happens when plastic waste is thrown on the ground instead of bins. This harms public spaces and reduces the beauty and cleanliness of the environment.

Overflowing bins

When bins are full and not emptied, plastic waste spills onto the ground. This spreads pollution and makes the surroundings unclean and unmanaged.
