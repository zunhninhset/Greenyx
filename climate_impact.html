Climate Impact

Plastic Waste and Climate Change

Plastic is made from fossil fuels. Its production and disposal release greenhouse gases. This contributes to global warming.

Greenhouse Gas Emissions

Making and burning plastic produces carbon dioxide and other gases. These gases trap heat in the atmosphere. This increases climate change.

Climate Impact on Ecosystems

Climate change affects land and ocean ecosystems. It leads to rising temperatures and extreme weather. This harms plants, animals, and biodiversity.
