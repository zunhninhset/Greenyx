What is Plastic Waste?

Plastic waste is any discarded, synthetic, or polymer-based material that has reached the end of its useful life.

Why plastic waste reduction matters?

It limit greenhouse gas emissions from fossil-fuel-based manufacturing, protects fragile marine and land ecosystems, and prevents toxic microplastics from entering the human food chain and vital organs.

Did you know?

Plastic waste is a major global issue. Most plastics are used for only a short time, but they remain in the environment for centuries, causing long-term pollution.
